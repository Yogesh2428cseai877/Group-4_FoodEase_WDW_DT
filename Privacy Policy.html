Privacy Policy

At FoodEase, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, contact details, delivery address, and payment information provided during order placement.
Non-Personal Information: Data like browser type, IP address, and pages visited to improve user experience.

How We Use Your Information

- To process and deliver orders efficiently.
- To communicate about your orders and send updates.
- To improve our services and website functionality.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your information to third parties. However, data may be shared with service providers involved in processing your orders (e.g., delivery services).

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information. Contact us at FoodEase@gmail.com for any privacy-related concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
